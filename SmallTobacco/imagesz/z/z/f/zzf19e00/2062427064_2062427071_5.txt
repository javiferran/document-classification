Ask First / It's the Law Additional Program Facts

In 47 states and the District of Columbia, it is against the law to sell tobacco products to anyone
under the age of 18.

In Alabama, Alaska and Utah, it is against the law to sell to anyone under the age of 19.

Kentucky, Missouri, and Oklahoma require by law state-specific Employee Acknowledgment
Forms that employees in these states must sign. Acknowledgment forms have been collated in
kits for these states to comply with these requirements. All other state kits include a generic
Employee Acknowledgment Form that we recommend retailers use with their employees.
