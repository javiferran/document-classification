Any future work to improve this product should include an investi-
gation having only 70~75% of the nicotine removed by using milder M-2000
processing conditions. Using a blend processed in this manner, a lower
"tar" product could be developed that would still yield nicotine levels
in the .10-.15 mg. range. This should reduce the off taste and off side-
stream aroma,

Some consideration should be given to incorporating M-2000 processed
flue-cured tobacco into low “tar" and nicotine cigarettes. This should
help in decreasing the nicotine, increasing pH, and yield a higher
flavored product.
