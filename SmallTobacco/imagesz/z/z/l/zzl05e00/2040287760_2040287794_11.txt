Seven out of the top ten couponed brands in 1988 again made the list in
1989. Dropping out of the top ten were Falcon Lights, Lucky Strike and
Raleigh, replaced by Alpine, Barclay and Richland 25’s. Further, except
for Vantage and Barclay, the top ten couponed brands are dominated by
Price Value entries.
