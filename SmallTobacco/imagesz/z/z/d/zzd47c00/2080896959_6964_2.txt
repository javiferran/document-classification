New Brand Launch-Initial Allowance

Please review the example below to understand how a wholesale account will be credited the initial
allowance. For Parliament Full Flavor and Lights Kings Box, this credit will not be paid ‘off-
invoice’, but instead will be paid via a weekly credit memo. If you have any questions concerning
this credit memo amount/process, please contact your Region Credit Manager in the New York office.

Example

Initial Allocation:

10 - 12M cases of Parliament Full Flavor Kings Box

10 - 12M cases of Parliament Full Flavor Men Kings Box
5 - 12M cases of Parliament Lights Kings Box

5 - 12M cases of Parliament Men Lights Kings Box.

30 Total 12M cases

3 - 12M cases of Parliament Full Flavor Men Kings Box
1 - 12M case of Parliament Lights Kings Box

1 - 12M case of Parliament Men Lights Kings Box

8 Total 12M cases

calculate how much product has been delivered to ABC Wholesale during the previous week and will
compare it to the initial allocation.
